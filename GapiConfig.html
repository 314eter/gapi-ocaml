Module GapiConfig


module GapiConfig: sig .. end
Configuration module.

Configures Ocurl and authorization method.

| Standard (*Standard debug function that prints everything on standard output.*)

type auth_config =
| NoAuth (*No authorization.*)
| ClientLogin of client_login_config (*Client Login.*)
| OAuth1 of oauth1_config (*OAuth1.*)
| OAuth2 of oauth2_config (*OAuth2.*)
Authorization method.
